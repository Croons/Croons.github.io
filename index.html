About Me

I'm Oscar, a game designer with a passion for creating immersive experiences. With expertise in Unity and Godot, I specialize in game design such as gameplay and features, but also XR development.

My approach combines innovative gameplay mechanics with thoughtful design to create engaging player and customer experiences.

I've been interested in software my whole life, with experience in both indie and professional scenes.

I love exploring new technologies and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in interactive media. Working across various platforms has given me a versatile skill set that allows me to adapt to different project requirements.

My goal is to create memorable experiences that engage and delight users while solving meaningful problems through thoughtful design and technical innovation.
